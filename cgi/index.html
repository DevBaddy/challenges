About AGDC

AGDC is a team enlisted by CGI to solve the problems that are happening in the world. We try to solve humanatarian crises with the help of Unicef. We solve wars that happen all around the world. We solve famines that happen by donating food to food banks all across the world. We believe that by 2022, we will solve about 99% of the problems in the world. We are here to solve problems. We have great decision makers who usually succeed in solving problems. 69 of them have won the Nobel Peace Prize.

Diversity & Inclusion

Here at AGDC, we want to have a diverse enviorment where anyone can work. We will work at a very civilized office space and insure that everyone will be kind to each other. We will tackle each problem that happens in the space and teach them about diversity. Diversity is our number 1 priority in this workspace. We welcome everyone, we don't discriminate people based on their race, sexuality, skin color, etc.
